Features

Built for how you experience film

◐

Gentle Recognition

No streaks that shame you. Just quiet acknowledgment when you've found a rhythm — like noticing you've watched films three weekends in a row.

◎

Variety Progress

Track exploration, not just quantity. See how many genres, decades, and countries you've visited this quarter. Resets fresh each season.

◇

Cinema Quests

Optional micro-challenges that feel like suggestions from a friend. "Try a documentary this week." Complete a few, earn a simple memento.

✦

Custom Rubrics

Rate films on your terms. Create weighted categories like Direction, Cinematography, Emotional Impact — or build your own from scratch.

≡

Ranked Lists

Build and share ranked lists of your favorite films. Drag to reorder. Perfect for "Best of the Year" or "Films That Made Me Cry."

☽

Mood & Moment

Capture how a film made you feel and when you watched it. Your data powers quirky year-end stats like "Most Late-Night Watch."

Getting Started

Three steps to cinematic joy

1

Log a Film

Add the title, director, and year. Choose a mood. Rate it your way — simple 1-10 or detailed rubric.

2

Build Your Taste

Over time, your diary reveals patterns. Directors you love. Decades you gravitate toward. Moods you chase.

3

Share & Compete

Create lists, join fantasy leagues, and discover films through friends who see cinema the way you do.

Fantasy Film League

Draft films releasing this year and compete with friends. Earn points through the quarterly regular season, then battle it out in the playoffs: Golden Globes and the Oscar Championship.
